# Portrait assets — credits

Portrait artwork in `source/` (and its processed copies in
`app/public/portraits/`) comes from **Portraits of the Rim** by
**TwoPenny** (TwoPennyDoodle).

- Nexus Mods: https://www.nexusmods.com/rimworld/mods/425
- Author page: https://twopennydoodle.com/portraits

Assets are used with attribution, as permitted by the mod page's
permissions. Originals are 600×600 PNG; processed copies (320×320 WebP)
are generated by `app/scripts/build-portraits.mjs`.

## Notes on curation

- **Face layers are split into `face-inner/` and `face-outer/`.**
  InnerFace and OuterFace are not visually identical: InnerFace is the
  colored skin-tone/detail layer (eyes, lips, skin coloring) and
  OuterFace is a black line-art overlay (eyebrow/eye/nose/mouth
  outlines). Both layers are required to render a complete face;
  downstream tasks should composite `face-inner` (tinted with the
  character's skin tone) underneath `face-outer` (no tint).
- Clothes were picked from across all bundled mod packs (Vanilla,
  Vanilla Expanded Apparel/Armour/Medieval/Settlers), using the
  `-m` (medium) build size, matching each profession to the closest
  visual garment. See `assets/portraits/source/clothes/` for the
  profession → garment mapping.
